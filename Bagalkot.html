Top Tourist Places in Bagalkot

Aihole

Cradle of Indian temple architecture featuring over 120 ancient temples built by the Chalukyas.

Badami Cave Temples

Rock-cut cave temples carved into red sandstone cliffs overlooking Agastya Lake.

Pattadakal

UNESCO World Heritage Site blending Dravidian and Nagara architectural styles.

Banashankari Temple

Historic temple dedicated to Goddess Banashankari near Badami.

Galaganatha Temple

Elegant Nagara-style temple within the Pattadakal complex.

Bhootanatha Temple

Scenic temple on the banks of Agastya Lake dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Kudala Sangama

Sacred confluence of Krishna and Malaprabha rivers.

Almatti Dam

Major dam across the Krishna River offering scenic sunset views.
